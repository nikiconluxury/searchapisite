Understanding Your Credits

What Are Credits?

Credits represent your usage allowance for API calls. Each plan comes with a predefined number of credits per month, ensuring you have the necessary resources to meet your data extraction needs.

Overage Policy

If you exceed your monthly credit allocation, you can purchase additional credits at a discounted rate. This ensures uninterrupted service and flexibility as your needs grow.

Tracking Usage

Monitor your credit usage in real-time through our dashboard. Stay informed about your consumption and optimize your data extraction strategies accordingly.
